How It Works — Inbound Email

  • Step 1: Someone sends email to hello@locavizion.com
  • Step 2: Cloudflare Email Routing intercepts it at the DNS level
  • Step 3: Cloudflare forwards it to your personal Gmail address
  • Step 4: Email appears in your Gmail inbox with "to: hello@locavizion.com" in the headers
  • Total cost: $0

How It Works — Outbound Email

  • Step 1: In Gmail, click Compose or Reply
  • Step 2: Click the "From" dropdown and select hello@locavizion.com
  • Step 3: Write and send your email normally
  • Step 4: Recipient sees the email as coming from hello@locavizion.com
  • This was configured via Gmail Settings → Accounts → "Send mail as"

Cloudflare Email Routing Setup

Already configured. If you ever need to add new addresses or change the forwarding destination:

  • Go to cloudflare.com → click locavizion.com
  • Click Email in the left sidebar → Email Routing
  • Under Routing rules you'll see all your configured addresses
  • Click Create address to add new ones (e.g. press@locavizion.com, investors@locavizion.com)
  • Each new address requires verifying your forwarding Gmail address (one-time — already done)

Gmail SMTP Configuration

To add a new "Send as" address in Gmail:

  • Go to Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings → Accounts and Import
  • Under "Send mail as" click Add another email address
  • Enter the name you want to appear (e.g. "Locavizion") and the address (e.g. press@locavizion.com)
  • Uncheck "Treat as an alias" if you want it to use its own SMTP
  • SMTP server: smtp.gmail.com, Port: 587, TLS: Yes
  • Username: your full Gmail address, Password: your Gmail app password (not your regular password — generate at myaccount.google.com → Security → 2-Step Verification → App passwords)
  • Click Add, verify via the confirmation email Gmail sends, done

Troubleshooting

Email shows as [email protected]
This is Cloudflare's email obfuscation. Go to Cloudflare → Scrape Shield → turn off Email Address Obfuscation. Or use JavaScript to assemble the email address (already implemented on the waitlist page).
Gmail "Send as" not working
Make sure you're using an App Password, not your regular Gmail password. Go to myaccount.google.com → Security → 2-Step Verification → App Passwords → generate a new one for "Mail."
Emails going to spam
Set up SPF and DKIM records in Cloudflare DNS. SPF: add a TXT record for locavizion.com with value "v=spf1 include:_spf.google.com ~all". DKIM: follow Gmail's instructions for adding a DKIM TXT record.
