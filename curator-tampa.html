Tampa Bay Curator Guide

5 founding Curator roles with real Instagram accounts, organisations, and specific people to approach right now in Tampa Bay.

Why Tampa Bay First

Tampa Bay is a World Cup host city with matches at Raymond James Stadium. But beyond the matches, Tampa has two distinct audiences that need local guidance:

  • World Cup visitors — International fans arriving for matches with zero knowledge of Tampa. Many will be Latin American (Brazil, Argentina, Colombia, Mexico) and will connect instantly with Ybor City's heritage if someone shows them
  • New residents & transplants — Tampa adds thousands of new residents monthly. Remote workers, tech transplants, retirees, and military families. They arrive knowing nobody and spend months-to-years feeling like outsiders. Year-round revenue opportunity

The 5 Founding Roles

🍽
Role 1 — Food & Restaurant Curator
Priority Hire · Highest Revenue Potential
Knows the real Tampa food scene beyond chains and tourist spots. Hidden gems in every neighbourhood. New openings before they're on Eater. Chef relationships. The person locals DM when they need a restaurant recommendation for a specific occasion.

Real Targets — Food

  • @tampafoodgirl — 33,000+ followers. Posts detailed Tampa restaurant reviews with photos. Professional content quality. Engages with followers in comments. Approach via Instagram DM referencing her most recent post specifically
  • @thisbabeeats — 57,000+ followers. Video-first food content. High engagement rate. Shows personality in reviews. Covers the broader Tampa Bay area including St. Pete. Approach via Instagram DM — lead with the 70% split and World Cup audience
  • Tampa Bay Food Bloggers Facebook Group — Active community of 2,000+ local food content creators. Post the opportunity as a group message, not a cold DM
  • Local restaurant managers — Talk to managers at places like Ulele, Bern's Steak House, Rooster & the Till, Columbia Restaurant. Ask "who do you think knows Tampa food better than anyone?"
💻
Role 2 — Tech & Startup Curator
Founding Role · Conference Audience
Connected to the Tampa tech ecosystem — accelerators, VCs, co-working spaces, startup events. Knows which founders are doing interesting work, which companies are hiring, where the community actually gathers. Can brief visiting founders and investors on who to meet.

Real Targets — Tech

  • Embarc Collective — Tampa's premier startup hub. Contact the community manager directly. They know everyone in the ecosystem and may have staff or alumni interested. Email community@embarccollective.com
  • Tampa Bay Wave — Accelerator program with deep alumni network. Reach out to recent cohort graduates who are locally connected but looking for additional income streams
  • Synapse Summit speakers — Annual Tampa innovation conference. Browse past speaker lists for people with strong Tampa ties and public-facing presence
  • Station House — St. Pete co-working space with an active community. Talk to the space manager about posting the opportunity
🎨
Role 3 — Arts & Culture Curator (St. Petersburg)
Founding Role · St. Pete Focus
Gallery openings, Dali Museum, SHINE Mural Festival, First Friday Art Walk. Knows the creative community personally — which artists are showing, which galleries matter, where the real creative energy is. St. Pete's art scene is world-class but underknown nationally.

Real Targets — Arts

  • Creative Pinellas — Pinellas County's arts council. They fund and promote local artists. Contact program directors for introductions to connected cultural figures
  • Morean Arts Center — Major St. Pete art institution. Staff or board members may know the right person
  • First Friday Art Walk organisers — Monthly St. Pete event that draws thousands. Whoever organises vendor participation and gallery coordination is deeply connected
  • SHINE Mural Festival — Annual international mural festival. The organising team knows every artist in the city
  • Local gallery owners on Central Ave — Walk Central Avenue in St. Pete and talk to 5 gallery owners. Ask "who here knows the art scene better than anyone?"
🇨🇺
Role 4 — Latin Heritage & Ybor City Curator
Founding Role · World Cup Critical
Deep knowledge of Tampa's Latin roots — the cigar industry history, Ybor City's founding families, Circulo Cubano, the Columbia Restaurant legacy, modern Latin culture. This Curator will be the most important person for Brazilian, Argentine, and Colombian fans visiting Tampa.

Real Targets — Latin Heritage

  • Ybor City Chamber of Commerce — Start here. They know every business owner, historian, and cultural figure in the district
  • Circulo Cubano — Historic Cuban club in Ybor. Contact board members or event coordinators
  • Columbia Restaurant family/management — The oldest restaurant in Florida. Their staff includes people with deep Ybor knowledge spanning generations
  • Tampa Bay History Center — Museum staff specialising in cigar industry and immigrant heritage. Academic knowledge plus community connections
  • Ybor City walking tour guides — People already doing historical tours. They may be interested in expanding into a more personalised, higher-paying Curator role
🚣
Role 5 — Outdoors & Waterfront Curator
Founding Role · Year-Round Revenue
Tampa Bay's waterfront is its greatest asset and most underappreciated feature for visitors. Kayaking, paddleboarding, manatee encounters, Bayshore Boulevard, beach access, nature trails, fishing. This Curator shows visitors the physical landscape that makes Tampa Bay special.

Real Targets — Outdoors

  • Tampa Bay kayak/paddleboard operators — Search Google Maps for "kayak Tampa Bay" — the operators with the best reviews are often passionate locals who'd love a platform to reach more people
  • Meetup.com Tampa outdoor groups — Groups like "Tampa Bay Outdoor Adventures" and "Tampa Bay Hiking" have organisers who are naturally community-oriented and have existing audiences
  • Nature photographers with Instagram followings — Search #TampaBayNature, #TampaBayPhotography. People with 5K-20K followers who post consistently are ideal — they love the outdoors and already create content
  • Weedon Island Preserve volunteers — Passionate about Tampa's natural environment. Connected to conservation community
