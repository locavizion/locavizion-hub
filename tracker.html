Weekly Review Template

Every Sunday evening, spend 20 minutes filling in this template:

Week of [date]

Numbers:
• Waitlist: [X] total (+[Y] this week)
• Curators: [X] conversations / [Y] signed
• Instagram: [X] followers (+[Y])
• Website: [X] unique visitors
• Chatbot: [X] conversations

Wins:
• [What went well]

Blockers:
• [What's stuck]

Next week priorities (max 3):
1. [Most important thing]
2. [Second priority]
3. [Third priority]
The Rule

If you can't fill in this template in 20 minutes, you're tracking too many things. Simplify. The only metrics that matter right now are: waitlist signups, Curator conversations, and user interviews. Everything else is noise until you have product-market fit evidence.
