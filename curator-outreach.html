Curator Outreach Playbook

Complete recruitment playbook — Tampa, Miami, NYC targets with real accounts. 8 copy-ready message templates. Pitch angles by personality type. Follow-up sequences. Full action checklist.

The Core Pitch — What Curators Get

  • 70% of every experience booking — Most food tour companies and experience platforms take 50%+. Locavizion gives Curators the majority because their knowledge is the product
  • 65% of every briefing session — 1:1 video calls with travelers, priced at the Curator's own rate ($50–$150/session), on the Curator's own schedule
  • Founding Curator title — Only 5 per city. Permanent position. First pick of traveler matches. Featured placement in the AI guide. Profile prominence
  • Full platform support — Locavizion handles payments (Stripe), scheduling (Calendly), marketing (Instagram, SEO, AI guide referrals), and customer support. Curators just show up and share what they know
  • World Cup audience — 10 million international visitors arriving in June-July 2026 with zero local knowledge, actively looking for guidance. The customer acquisition is built in
  • No upfront costs — No subscription fee, no monthly minimum, no equipment to buy. You earn when you work

6 Pitch Angles — By Curator Personality

Angle 1 — The Entrepreneur
For people who already think of their knowledge as a business. Lead with economics: "70% split, you set your rate, you design your experiences, you own your schedule." Emphasise the founding title as equity-like value. These people respond to revenue projections and scale potential.
Angle 2 — The Passionate Local
For people who genuinely love their city and want to share it. Lead with mission: "You know things about this city that visitors will never find on Google. The World Cup is bringing millions of people who need exactly what you know." These people respond to purpose and impact, not just money.
Angle 3 — The Content Creator
For Instagram/TikTok food bloggers, travel creators, lifestyle influencers. Lead with audience: "You already create content about this city. Locavizion turns that content into bookable experiences. Your followers become your customers. We handle payment, you handle the magic." These people respond to audience growth and monetisation.
Angle 4 — The Industry Insider
For restaurant managers, hotel concierges, event planners, bar owners. Lead with access: "You have connections that money can't buy. Your rolodex is the product. Imagine getting paid every time someone asks 'where should I eat?' and you actually have the answer." These people respond to leveraging existing networks.
Angle 5 — The Community Builder
For accelerator managers, co-working space operators, cultural org leaders. Lead with community: "The World Cup is bringing founders, investors, and business travelers who want to connect. You already know everyone. This is about making sure the right people find each other." These people respond to ecosystem impact.
Angle 6 — The Cultural Ambassador
For people with deep cultural roots — Latin heritage in Ybor City, African diaspora in Harlem, immigrant community leaders. Lead with representation: "A million fans from your country are coming to this city. Without someone like you, they'll experience the tourist version. You can change that." These people respond to cultural pride and representation.
